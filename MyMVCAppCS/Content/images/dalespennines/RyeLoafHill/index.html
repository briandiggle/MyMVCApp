Rye Loaf Hill and Grizedales from Settle

6 November 2007

Settle railway station -> Grizedales -> Rye Loaf Hill -> Settle

"Wait in Jerusalem until you have been clothed with power from
on high". 2008 - a time to wait on God and come in to the fulness
of the Spirit.

In Banjul 1994, God spoke to me as I left Africa about the fulness
of the Spirit. Now I do not want to return to West Africa without
this fulness. I must walk in the power of the Spirit in Senegal.

I don't want to return from my exile empty. A want to be like
Jacob returning to the land of his inheritance with great possessions.
I want to walk there in the promise of Job 29- in the cream of His
Word, where the Rock pours out for me streams of olive oil. Now
is the time for the fulness of this promise to come - "when my
path was drenched with cream".

This year, let a change take place in me. Let me come through as
Jacob, in weakness and dependency on God, in brokenness before
Him "always carrying around in my body the death of Jesus, that
the life of Jesus may be manifested in me"- in the power of the Spirit.
