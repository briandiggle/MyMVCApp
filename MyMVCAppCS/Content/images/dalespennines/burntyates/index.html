Nidderdale - Killinghall to South Stainley

20th July 2004

Killinghall (Three Horse shoes) 09:45am ,Otley Road, Crag Lane, FP to Myers Green, FP Hampsthwaite, Over bridge, FP crossing disused railway towards Clint, intersecting with Church Lane, crossing Clint bank taking FP past ruined Clint Hall, FP to west house farm, FP (FT not clear) to Dinmore Ho via Monk Wall, FP NE to Burnt Yates, Lovely Inn at Burnt Yates (W) closed!, Lunch in inn at Burnt Yates(E), Backtract to Burnt Yates(W) and taking FP N to Shaw Mills, follow Nidderdale Way East then N up Scarah Bank, through Barsneb wood (Get lost at this point and use compass to get back on track through woods. Follow track E from High Cayton to A61/South Stainley bus stop 16:45pm.
