High Crag Ridge, Nidderdale

1085ft

c. 20 May 2002

Parked at Summerbridge 10:20am

Walked along nidderdale way through dacre banks until footpath
forks towards Bryans wood. (bluebells!)
Take footpath to follow smelt maria dyke up, dyke on the left.
Take footpath straight to Heyshaw (get lost just before heyshaw
and jump into swill!)
Followed C road out of Heyshaw, then 1st right. Took 1st footpath
on R. Climbed High Crag Ridge (1085Ft), got stone from top!
Plunged down post mobile phone tower into Bark Cabin Wood. Surprise
about 100 rabbits sunning themselves in a cove.
Found Guisecliff Tarn, serene, but no time to stop! Carpets
of bluebells..
Head for Baylis gap, pass kamikaze sheep.. See a weasel? Walk on C roads into
pateley bridge (cross bridge at 12:59, 1 minute ahead of schedule)

Lunch at the crown.

Steep ascent on other side of valley heading for scot ash gate, pass
small revervoir and laverock hall before hitting the road, turning R
First left, then short cut using footpath (Again unmarked, as many have
been today)
Turn R, and long haul down towards brimham rocks. Almost stumble on
an owl feasing on a dead bird. Binocular brought into play!
