Loadpot Hill

21 September 2008

"The fusedale horseshoe"  - a fantastic days walking.

Howtown- Steel Knotts - Brownthwaite Crag - Gowk Hill- Wether Hill - Loadpot Hill - Lords Seat - Arthur's Pike - Bonscale Pike - Howtown

Probably also Swarth Fell - need GPS track to confirm.

Met with God in a special way on Steel Knotts as God restored my trust in Him after the nightmare of the way Paris ended.
