When the toronto prophecy talked of me having "Moses' experiences" I didnt have in mind exactly what today was like. Probably more emotion and excitement. But I do feel today was one such time.

I left the valley feeling overwhelmed by all the things on my shoulders. I have worries, grieving and disappointments. Most of all, I have so many church expectations -eldership, regular preaching, regularly leading worship, supposedly co-ordinating evangelism, helping lead a housegroup. And to top this I should be focusing on the Big One soon to come.

As I left the valley I didnt leave my confusion and weariness behind. I sat down overlooking Brownehowe Bottom at Point 3 and was quickly enveloped in cloud. As set off for the summit and God spoke! I saw myself at work last week. I was about to tackle the SAP SSO for the portal and I knew I couldnt beging the "Big One" until I had cleared my desk. So I got all the little remaining bits of work out of the way and out of my head. Then I cleared my desk, went for a drink, and returned ready to start. I could feel with mental exhaustion of attempting to start the Big One when all the other bits were cluttering my desk and mind. This is where I am now. I need to clear my desk. One by one I need to put some of these present things to bed so I can be ready to start the Big One. In the space of 30 seconds my confusion had gone and I had peace! The rest of the walk was a great delight to just rest in God. Lord how wonderful it is when you speak - oh how I need to hear your voice, the voice of the wonderful counsellor.

The cloud persisted on Tarn Crag summit and only cleared on the summit of Grey Crag. Ticked off Great Howe also,a lovely grassy Hill and left a marker at NY 48893 06392 (elev 494m) waypoint name G7HOWM. Great Howe summit had no cairn only a grassy hump with a tuft of coarser grass.

Footnote:
As moses came off the mountain he was immediately confronted by mess of the valley! I drove to Brockhole where me, Mum and Emily had lunch. I ate a dogy pastie. By 4pm I was feeling queasy and by 10pm was sick. The next 36 hours were nasty!
