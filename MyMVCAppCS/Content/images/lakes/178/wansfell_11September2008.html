Wansfell and Wansfell Pike from Troutbeck

11 September 2008

Route and partial track

Written late June 2009 in Senegal: I remember this walk as the handkerchief walk as I seemed to develop a cold on the ascent which caused me to walk along the ridge with a handkerchief continually at hand. I was quite physically run-down when I did this walk after having just been through the demenagement from Paris and the search for a flat in Leeds.
