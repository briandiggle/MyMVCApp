Sour Howes, 25th May 2003

set off from Layby before limefitt entrance 7:20pm?
8:37pm at top
Layby before limefitt entrance 9:30pm


As seems to happen often at the moment, I've come away from Leeds
quite down emotionally and spiritually. Emily has not been sleeping
through and Linda's cold persists. I dont seem to have the energy
to get out of the valley and it was this walk which gave me the
hope to climb out again. I almost felt it was happening literally
as I climbed up from Limefitt park.

I lift up my eyes to the hills-
where does my help come from?
2 My help comes from the LORD ,
the Maker of heaven and earth.


This so often happens and is why I love to walk alone. Though I
know that there is a great difference between loneliness and solitude.
Solitude is being alone with God and I find that solitude in the mountains.

Parked in the layby just short of Limefitt park campsite/caravan park which
was heaving. It even has a pub! Soon left the crowds behind huddled in their
tents and struck directly for applethwaite quarry with its strange viewing
platforms. After negotiating a couple of barbed wire fences I was heading
for the summit. It was a lovely clear, cool evening, the weather having
broken suddenly at tea-time.
