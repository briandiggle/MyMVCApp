Lingmoor Fell

26.12.2000 From Chapel Stile

This was a great walk! I was surprised at the what this little fell offers, but it has a great position in the middle of an amphitheatre of fells. A crystal clear december day helped. Walking from chapel stile gives a varied experience of river walking, slate quarry, forest, and ridge with few dull moments. The afternoon sunshine made this very pleasant. Coming off the fell at sunset, Wetherlam looked very grand the other side of the ridge the fairfield range was pink as the sunset hit the snow. One of my favourites.

Disturbed on the summit by a holiday making couple with baby coming up at me unawares from Blea Tarn
