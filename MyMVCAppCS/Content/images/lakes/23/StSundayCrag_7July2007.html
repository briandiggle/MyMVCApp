St. Sunday Crag

7th July 2007

Patterdale 15:00-> Arnison Crag 15:37-> Birks 16:42-> St. Sunday Crag 18:10-> Gavel Pike 19:07-> Lords Seat 19:46-> Deepdale -> Patterdale

Things I can remember of this walk from September 2009:

The Lord was speaking to me on the ascent of Arnison Crag about language learning - always remember that language is only
the facilitator - it is what is stored up within and is released that counts/

Spent a good deal of time lingering on St Sunday Crag summit as the sun was lowering - no-one around. One leg started to hurt
on the descent off Gavel pike which got worse as time went on, making the going quite difficult by the time I got into the valley.

This walk took place a week before the WEC interview on the 15th. 10 days later we were accepted onto the candidates
course for January 2008
