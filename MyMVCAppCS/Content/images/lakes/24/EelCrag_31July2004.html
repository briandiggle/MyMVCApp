Eel Crag (Crag Hill)

31 July 2004

 

Braithwaite -> Barrow -> Stile End ->Outerside -> Sail -> Eel Crag -> Hope Gill Head -> Grizedale Pike

Wonderful walk in near-perfect conditions when God spoke SO much! God spoke to me on the ascent of each peak
and I wrote down what He was saying on each Summit! Markers left.

Walked with God.
