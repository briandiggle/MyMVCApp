High Raise - Martindale/ Rampsgill horseshoe

29 December 2004

16.7Km. Moving Average 3.6kmh. Overall Speed 2.2kmh

Sunrise 08:32 Sunset 15:43

Total time 6 hrs 55 mins. Time Moving 4 hrs 45 mins

Martindale church 08:50 - Wether Hill 10:30 (Marker Left)- High Raise (Marker Left) 11:45 - Kidsty Pike 12:20 - Rampsgill Head 12:40
The Knott 13:00 - Rest Dodd (Marker Left) 13:30 - The Nab (Marker Left) 14:15 - Martindale church 15:55

First walk with a GPS. Really helped up on the tops with snow lying and high wind raging. 

(Memories as at Sept 2009: Wind raging particularly on Kidsty Pike and The Knott. Saw a red squirrel at the REDSQL marker.
Wasn't able to cross over wall to take in Red Crag. Found the "Fallen Tree" marker, now absent, which was my
first geocache found on my own. 

Met this guy wandering north near high raise, looking for a way to mardale. He had a GPS but didn't know how
to use it! Directed him to take Randale Beck down - wonder if he made it? Other than that there was a group
of teenagers on The Knott trying to stand on walls in the wind, doing alot of damage to them in the process.
