Stony cove pike

4 June 2004

From Kirkstone Pass Inn 7:30pm -> Stony cove pike 9:00am -> Kirkstone Pass Inn 10:15pm

6.5Km, 356m ascent

 

From Kirkstone pass, 7:30pm. Awesome clear evening and sunset over Catstycam

I came into this walk quite close to God and it was a time of just pouring out my heart to Him in joy at what he has done and the glory my eyes were seeing. The Lord reminded me of the Joy of the Holy Spirit, about Holy laughter and encouraged me that as I seek Him I will know more and more of this:

"You love righteousness and hate wickedneess therefore the Lord...has anointed you with the oil of Joy"

I also had a sense of how this period of time is/ has been pivotal in the direction of my life as I was reminded of the scripture "Stand at the crossroads and look..". It is so important for me to pres into God at this time, to fill my life with "secret glory". The open door to the nations is now much nearer.

This was such a memorable walk! As the sunset came, each few minutes brought a different wonder. In the sky sudden cloud formations were lit up, to the west over catsycam the sun went down thought this layers of cloud. After this, indescribably delicate pinks and oranges over the Kentmere fells. Finally when I thought that the show was over a glorious shade of orange merging to dark blue was framed though the pass towards Place Fell.

For much of the evening, Red Screes loomed darkly to the South West, yet at one point near the Atkinson memorial cross, its edge was brought out by the setting sun in a ribbon of verdant green.

As the glorious unfolding were coming to an end, I mixed stillness and awe with burst of fell running as my feet became like the feet of a deer.
