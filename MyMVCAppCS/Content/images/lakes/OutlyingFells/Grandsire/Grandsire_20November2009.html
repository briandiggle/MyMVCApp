Grandsire from near Ings

20 November 2009

First walk after returning from Senegal. Waved goodbye to Grandsire on the train from widermere on 31st March thinking it would be much longer before I would see it again...

C Road from ings parking -> Whasdyke  -> Grandsire -> Yews -> Parking

First section of GPS track before battery gave out

Grandsire became synonymous with the "overseas mission" season of my life, and inextricably associated with WEC. The first ascent in May 2007 was made just after we had committed ourselves to walking with WEC. The second ascent here took place just after leaving WEC Senegal. The walk saw me grappling with confusion, anger at WEC and anger at God. How could God allow us to waste all that time and effort on an organisation well known to be controlling? How could it be that we only discovered this in Senegal?

The consolation of a broken call to overseas mission is the chance to wander the fells and dales of the UK during my 40's.

Back now for three days, this was the first opportunity to get out after virus and biblical proportion floods hit combria. Burneside even got on national TV!
