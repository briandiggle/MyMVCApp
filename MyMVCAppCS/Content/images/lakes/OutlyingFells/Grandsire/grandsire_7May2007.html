Grandsire from near Ings

7 May 2007

C Road from ings parking 07:35 -> Grandsire 08:15 -> Parking

First section of GPS track before battery gave out

A new addition to the "before breakfast" collection of walks I have begun to put together
recently! When (as today) opportunities are limited for a more serious outing, I've found
these really rewarding.

Todays walk included a beautiful section of semi-ancient woodland (near Whasdike) which
was the highlight of the time - morning sun shining through the fresh green leaves of May onto
a carpet of bluebells must be hard to beat anywhere in the world. These are the sorts of
images and places I will miss alot when I'm in mission.
