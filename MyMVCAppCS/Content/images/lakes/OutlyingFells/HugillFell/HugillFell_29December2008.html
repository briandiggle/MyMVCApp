Hugill Fell, 29 December 2008

Parking at the car parking area next to the river, just past the bridge at the start of the kentmere road in Staveley.

Another fantastic christmas breakfast walk! Not much time in christmas this year as we have to return for Linda's scan on the 31st. Clear skies, crisp frost, sunrise. Better visibility even than yesterday. The whole lake district without a single cloud. The biggies -scafell range- clear in the distance. Surprised to find a clear path all the way from the road to the summit. Today did come across someone - a fellrunner and his dog - who I noticed completing a quick tour of Reston scan, the "unnamed summit at 273m" and Hugill Fell.

A great place to get a taste of a fell experience -heather, bracken, a little rock, and decent summit! After this, ready for a day with the 3 kids back at Kendal.
