Hugill Fell, 2 January 2010

Possibly the last chance to do a walk before taking the plunge once again into a new life, like February 1994 except this time with wife and daughter. That time I really did go empty with little more than the clothes on my back. Quick decision at 3pm to jump in the car before darkness came. Snow still lying an inch deep on the ground, weather still bitterly cold. Parking at the car parking area next to the river, just past the bridge at the start of the kentmere road in Staveley.

Extended the walk to take in the un-named summit at 273m.

Praying about the return to Leeds, felt the Lord saying to me that I should live more in the present and deal with the days own troubles and challenges. The question of returning to MVBC looms but I left that it was not something the Lord wanted to speak to me about today but that that is "tomorrows trouble".
