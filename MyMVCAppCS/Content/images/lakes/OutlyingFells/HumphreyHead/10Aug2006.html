Humphrey Head

10th August 2006

Escaping the noise and clamour of Abbott Hall family week! Emily is in bed. 15 minutes later I'm at the North Yorkshire CC outward bound centre.

This is a time of dealing with an embitterred heart with God. Dreams for the first time appear dead. Trust in God as the dream giver has been shaken. In my face everywhere the effortless blessing of others. Yet still I am here. In my hand is nothing for which I laid down family. We're immersed in "family" culture which screams "leper" to those who have only one child. We are, as "Duckys Park Farm, Flookburgh" insisted "2 adults and 1 child, not a family".
