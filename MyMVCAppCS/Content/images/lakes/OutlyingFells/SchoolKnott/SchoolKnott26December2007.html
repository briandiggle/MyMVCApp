School Knott, 26 December 2007

Date

The only walk managed in this period of intense activity in preparation for mission. Shortly after this walk I had a very nasty virus, the third in the last two months - an indicator the pressure I have been under recently.

An attempt to place a marker in the centre of the grass summit was going well until a fell-running suddely appeared as if from nowhere and took a keen interest in what I was doing. Once I had made it clear that he should mind his own business, he continue. Lesson learnt though- stick to the rules of placement and things work better. This I did afterwards and left a small marker in the usual place.
