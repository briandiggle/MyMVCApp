Brunt Knott

30 December 2006

Hall Lane Jnc 08:26 -> 09:06 Brunt Knott Summit-> Leave Brunt Knott 09:26 -> Hall Lane Jnc 09:50

Marker left.

After the first successful pre-breakfast walk from Kendal,
I decided to tackle somethin just a little bigger. And with
my new-found interest in Birketts, this was helpfully placed.

The media was insisting on severe weather over the new
year period. New years eve was supposed to have severe gales
and rain! And getting up in the dark I could hear the rain
beating on the window. I decided to go for it anyway and
was rewarded!

The weather on the ascent was "squally" - showers could be
seen approaching over from the stavely area giving time for
waterproofs to be on before the rain began.

I was given a treat on the summit as the weather cleared
all around, finally bathing me in sunshine. The gray-green of
the ascent changed into the most beautifully verdant green of
morning sunlight on the fields below the Knott.

I felt the Lord calling me once again to intimacy with me,
to take delight in friendship with Him. As the door to the
nations opens and effective service beckons, He wants to
always be my first delight. I must always seek His face, not
just delight in service.
