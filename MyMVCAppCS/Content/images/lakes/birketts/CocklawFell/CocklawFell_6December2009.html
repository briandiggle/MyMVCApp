Cocklaw Fell

6 December 2009

Stockdale Bridge - > Bridleway -> Cocklaw Fell -> Skeggles Water -> Track -> Black Crag -> Underhill House -> Stockdale Bridge

Average Speed: 2.8 km/h
Total Time: 02:14:13
Cartographic Length = 6.283 km

It wasn't throwing it down - good enough - so off I went. Linda and Em going to the Christingle service at Stricklandgate. We went this am - first visit to Church that I can remember since the "Look at my calling" preacher at DA. Positive experience actually with one person having been through a similar setback and confirming that "nothing is wasted with God". Two others saw our decision are courageous rather than cowardly. It was good to have a posotive experience of God's people again in a format setting.

During the walk prayed through the fact that we have had a near escape from sprititual abuse and the effect that this has had upon us.
