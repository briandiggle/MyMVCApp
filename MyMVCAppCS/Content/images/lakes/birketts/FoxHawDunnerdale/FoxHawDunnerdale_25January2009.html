Dunnerdale fells curcuit

25 January 2009

Broughton Mills 10:35 -> Little Stickle 11:37-> Great Stickle(M) 11:52 -> Tarn Hill 12:38 -> Stickle Pike 13:07-> Fox Haw 14:21-> Brown Haw  14:42 -> Fox Haw 14:54 -> Ravens Crag 15:41-> The Knott 16:22 -> Broughton Mills 16:55

Average speed:  1.6Kmh
Total Time: 6 hours 20 mins
Distance: 10.534Km

Weather started bright, overcast with some rain, then cleared at around 2pm. Originally the intention was to use Caw as the focal point of the day. But it became evident, when looking at the mass of Caw in front of me when descending towards it off Fox Haw that there was no enough daylight left for this.
