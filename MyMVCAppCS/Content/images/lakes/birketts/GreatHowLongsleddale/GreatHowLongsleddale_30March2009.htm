Great How

30 March 2009

Final taste of the lakes before departing to the flat and dry of Dakar. Longsleddale on a cool spring evening with the sun setting over the coniston fells. No-one in sight.

Just finished reading "Le Hobbit" (17th April). These words from the song "The Road goes ever on and on" struch me particularly as I am here in the dust of Dakar, the lakes now beyond reach for over a year.

...Mais les pieds qui sont partis a l'aventure
Reviennent enfin au lointain foyer.
Les yeux qui le feu et l'epee ont vu
Et l'horreur dans les salles de pierre,
Se posent enfin sur les verts paturages
Et les arbres et les collines depuis longtemps connus.
