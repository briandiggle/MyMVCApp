Whiteside Pike

7 September 2008

Dryhowe Bridge 19:00-> Whiteside Pike summit 19:34-> Dryhowe Bridge 20:12

 

Returning unexpectedly from exile, this was the first opportunity I got to walk. Great to discover the hidden valley of Bannerdale - lonely and little frequented. This was an "atmospheric" walk, with some real atmospheric conditions to deal with, particularly the high winds and the fast-closing night.

It was also the first taste of photos from the new panasonic camera. A number had to be discarded due to the high winds making it very difficult to take a "still"!
