Borwick Knott

25 November 2009

Purple track below

Track of walk

One week in Kendal, so far the weather has been sooo bad that only quick excursions are possible before breakfast. So much rain that the national news is even asking people not to go on the fells!

This place is a little gem - far superior to Grandsire. One wonders why wainwright chose Grandsire over this. It is a little bit of lakeland rising above the farmland surroundings.  A real rocky summit , views of a number of little tarns, and long range views towards the howgills and pennines, something Grandsire cant boast.
