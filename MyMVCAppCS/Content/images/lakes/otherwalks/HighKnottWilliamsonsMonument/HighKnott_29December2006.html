High Knott (Williamsons Monument)

29 December 2006

Would it be possible to do a walk before breakfast. I mean a walk with a summit? The proof-of-concept had already taken place - reaching Helm Crag summit at 08:14 in the summer. But in the dark of winter?

Set off before dawn and parked at the "157" on the C road from Ings. Set off at 08:38 and was on top by 09:06. The weather wasnt kind and the day had hardly begun as I was on the summit. The monument provided welcome shelter on its Eastern side from the high winds blowing. Skirted away from being seen from "The Heights" on the way up, but was
more brave on the way down. Actually the higher end of the farm was a building site - conversion into accomodation.

Great sense of accomplishment though as I arrived back at Applerigg at 10:10 to see pyamas and bleary eyes and the inward senses of "I've climbed a mountain and you havent even had breakfast"!
