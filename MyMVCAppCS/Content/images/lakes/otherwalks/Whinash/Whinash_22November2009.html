Whinash, 22 November 2009

The atrocious weather continues which began almost upon our return from Senegal. This Sunday morning it soon became clear that I was either to go to church on my own or sit watching CBeebies for two hours. So despite the lashing rain, I decided a walk was a better option.

Somewhere wild, unexplored. This was the place.

No cairn on summit but was happy to glance at the GPS as it briefly showed 471m.

Gale force winds on the top. Rain was intermittent. Worst section was the driving hail which thankfully lasted just a few minutes.

Length: 5.6Km
Average Speed: 3.1Km/h
Time: 1 hour 47m
