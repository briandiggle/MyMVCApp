Kirkhead Tower nr Abbott Hall

11 August 2006

The second attempt and reaching the summit of this little hill above Abbott Hall. The old path has long been forgotten and now only hints as from an ancient past remain. Straight up then from the tennis courts, past an iron-fenced enclosure with some impressive mature pines, over a wall. Here a new path can be found, well used, leading in the direction of Allithwaite.

The tower is a good viewpoint for the peninsula. It was also a refuge from some very inquisitive cows and a horse!

Marker was left in the Eastern facing wall, half way along about 2 feet above the ground.
