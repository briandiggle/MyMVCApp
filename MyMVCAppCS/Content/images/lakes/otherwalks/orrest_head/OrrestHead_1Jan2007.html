Orrest Head

1 January 2007

With Mum and Emily

Good way to start the year and Emily's first "Dadda mountain"!

Linda was taking it easy at Kendal. Mum, a creature of habit, fancied
a new-years day ascent of Orrest Head. As the weather was offering
a brief suggestion of hope, we set off! Emily had no trouble climbing
the mountain and had good fun scrambling about on the rocks at the
summit. We beat a hasty retreat though as the weather began to close
in.

Views from the top were fairly extensive, though the high fells
were in mist.

Emily was busy showing off her climbing ability to a crowd of
onlookers when she fell unceremoniously onto her backside and generated
a good roar of laughter. She saw the funny side of it for about
3 seconds and then found the reality that she was the object of
their amusement hard to bear. At this point the crowd quickly
melted away and Emily descended in a mork which lasted until half
way down the descent!
