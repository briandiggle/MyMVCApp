Ben More, Isle of Mull

26 August 2004

Dhiseig -> Summit ridge (c. 2800 feet) -> Dhiseig

With Christine

Chose the worst day for an attempt! Lack of proper equipment cost us. Weather closed in soon after
began the walk. By the time the summit ridge was reached, howling wind and driving rain made it quite
unpleasant. At this point christines' leg starts to go numb. Without waterproof trouser, her short kagool
was just moving all the cold water onto her legs. We had just passed a point in which the path passes
through a small chicane between two outcrops of rock (only about 3 feet high).
