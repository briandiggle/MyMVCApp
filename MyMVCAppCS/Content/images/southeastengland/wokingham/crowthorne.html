Approx 15km 17:20 Crowthorne -> 22:02 South Lodge

A wonderful walk, crowned by a time in which the presence of the Holy Spirit was so strong I
could not walk!

Arriving at crowthorne station, the heat of the day was just giving way to the gentle warmth of
a balmy summer evening. The intended walk was interrupted initially when I discovered my GPS
to have only minutes left of battery. A detour into the outskirts of sandhurst was necessary to
get the batteries. Back on course I began to share my heart with the Lord. Soon I entered into
the peace of being in touch with God. I poured out my heart to Him about my present battles.

Then came the voice of the Holy Spirit as I was taking a rest at point A -

"I will set you free that you may spend your strength on the poor"

Hope flooded my spirit for the future, where I would see all the promises he has spoken to
me and linda fulfilled. From this point onwards I had such peace!

My route took me into finchampsteam ridge, a lovely area of mixed woodland, taking in a
geocache. Here also an avenue of 130-year old Wellingtonia (Giant Sequioa) trees.

But I will always remember the time when the presence of the Holy Spirit was so heavy
and sweet upon me that I literally was not able to walk properly. For 45 minutes from
points 16 to 18, I stumbled forwards slowly in His Holy presence. It was not necessary
or appropriate to speak.

At the end of this section, I asked the Lord for my legs back so that I could get home
before dark!
