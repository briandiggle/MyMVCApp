Shinfield to Wokingham

28 June 2005

Took the 144 from Wokingham Broad Street at 17:27 and arrived at Shinfield School Green at 18:00.

The weather had been threatening to break for all day, but had not even when I got the bus! The
rain then started on the journey to Shinfield and built up to a fairly major thunderstorm and
downpour just as I exited The Coombes.

I had some fun in the last section of the walk as the FP I was expecting to take was no-where
to be seen. I ended up wading through tall grass and undergrowth to head straight for the railway
brigde. I turned up a South Lodge absolutely soaking from the waist down!
